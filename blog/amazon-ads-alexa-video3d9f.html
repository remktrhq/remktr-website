Amazon says it turns your brand into digital wallpaper—always around, not lost on a phone feed. Rachel Kaplan at Amazon Ads puts it this way: “It’s the living room, reimagined as a media touchpoint.” Ads aren’t in the way—they’re just a part of daily life.
