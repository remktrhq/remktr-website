Cracking the Code of FBM Ship+

At first glance, this update might seem like code mumbo jumbo. But dig underneath, and you find a tool that smooths out your order process. FBM Ship+ boosts how sellers fulfill their own orders. Why's that a big deal?

DIY Style: With Fulfillment By Merchant (FBM), you call the shots. FBA (Fulfillment By Amazon) stores your stuff, but FBM means you're in charge. And with FBM Ship+, you're even more the boss!

Jamie North, an e-commerce guru, says, "FBM Ship+ gives independent sellers more control and insight over shipping without leaning on Amazon’s system alone." This lets sellers tweak their shipping schedules and maybe save some cash.

Why FBM Ship+ is a Big Win for Sellers

First off, better tracking means better business. FBM Ship+ gives real-time updates on where your orders stand. Track every box from your place to the customer's door, using Amazon's tough-as-nails setup.

Think about this: studies show about 63% of folks want tracking when shopping online. With FBM Ship+, you're meeting that need, upping your odds of loyal, happy shoppers. This could switch a one-time shopper into a devoted fan.
