What's Happening: UNIX to ISO

Ah, the UNIX timestamp. Efficient, yes, but like peering into a coder's antique book—a stream of digits counting seconds since January 1, 1970. Precision is its game, yet its complexity is like reading ancient scripts.

In 2025, these UNIX timestamps will get a facelift for an ISO 8601 look in Amazon Japan. Timestamps like 2025-08-27T14:00:00+09:00 will become the norm. Why the switch? In a vast global market, dodging costly errors by avoiding misinterpretations makes good business sense.

For Amazon sellers who need clear scheduled deliveries, this means fewer headaches. So, get ready to embrace flexible parsing methods that handle both UNIX and ISO 8601, letting your systems speak both languages until the transition is complete.
